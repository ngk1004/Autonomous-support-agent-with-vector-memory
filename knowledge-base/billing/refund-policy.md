# Refund Policy

AcmeCloud offers refunds under the following conditions.

## Eligible Refunds

- **Duplicate charges**: If you were charged twice for the same billing period, we issue a full refund for the duplicate within 5–7 business days.
- **Service outage**: Credits or refunds apply when our SLA guarantees are not met (99.9% uptime). Outages must exceed 30 consecutive minutes.
- **Annual plan cancellation within 14 days**: Full refund if you cancel within 14 days of purchase and have used less than 10% of API quota.

## Non-Refundable

- Partial months on monthly plans after the first 14 days.
- Add-on marketplace integrations purchased separately.
- Overages from API usage beyond your plan limit.

## How to Request a Refund

1. Open **Settings → Billing → Transaction History**.
2. Select the charge and click **Dispute Charge**.
3. Or email billing@acmecloud.example with your account email, invoice ID, and reason.

Refunds are processed to the original payment method. Bank processing may take 5–10 business days after approval.

## Duplicate Charge Investigation

When reporting a duplicate charge, include:

- Invoice IDs for both charges
- Date and amount of each charge
- Last four digits of the card used

Our billing team typically resolves duplicate charge cases within 2 business days.
