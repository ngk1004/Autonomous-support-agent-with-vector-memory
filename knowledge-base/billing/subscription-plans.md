# Subscription Plans and Billing Cycles

AcmeCloud offers three subscription tiers billed monthly or annually.

## Plans Overview

| Feature | Starter | Pro | Enterprise |
|---------|---------|-----|------------|
| Price (monthly) | $29/mo | $99/mo | Custom |
| Price (annual) | $290/yr | $990/yr | Custom |
| API calls/month | 50,000 | 500,000 | Unlimited |
| Team members | 3 | 25 | Unlimited |
| SSO | — | ✓ | ✓ |
| Support | Email | Priority email | Dedicated CSM |

## Upgrading

1. **Settings → Billing → Change Plan**
2. Select new plan
3. Prorated charge applies immediately
4. New limits take effect instantly

## Downgrading

Downgrades take effect at the **end of the current billing period**. You retain Pro features until then.

Warning: Downgrading may disable SSO and reduce team seat limits. Remove excess users before downgrade date.

## Annual vs Monthly

- Annual saves ~17% vs monthly
- Switch mid-cycle: contact billing@acmecloud.example for prorated adjustment

## Cancellation

Cancel anytime under **Settings → Billing → Cancel Subscription**. Access continues until period end. Data export available for 30 days after cancellation.
