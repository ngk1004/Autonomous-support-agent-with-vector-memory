# Tax and VAT on Invoices

AcmeCloud calculates sales tax and VAT based on your billing address and local regulations.

## US Sales Tax

Sales tax applies to customers in US states where AcmeCloud has tax nexus. Tax is calculated at checkout and shown on invoices.

Tax-exempt organizations: upload your exemption certificate under **Settings → Billing → Tax Exemption**.

## EU VAT

EU customers are charged VAT at their country's rate unless a valid VAT ID is provided.

1. **Settings → Billing → Tax Information**
2. Enter your VAT ID (e.g., `DE123456789`)
3. We validate via VIES; reverse charge applies if valid

## UK VAT

Post-Brexit UK VAT rules apply. Provide UK VAT number for B2B reverse charge eligibility.

## Invoice Tax Line Items

Invoices show:

- Subtotal (pre-tax)
- Tax rate and amount
- Total due

Download itemized invoices from **Settings → Billing → Invoices**.

## Updating Tax Information

Changes apply to **future invoices only**. Contact billing@acmecloud.example to reissue past invoices with corrected tax details.
